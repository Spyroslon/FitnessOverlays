How It Works

Simple steps to make your activities share-worthy.

1. Connect

Click the Strava button to securely link your account. We only access activity data.

Browse your recent activities and select the one you want to transform into an overlay.

3. Customize

Choose stats to show, pick colors, adjust text size, alignment, and columns for the perfect look.

4. Copy & Share

Copy the overlay image (transparent background) or text, and overlay it on your Images or Videos.
